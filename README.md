# Spicy Food Restaurant

A curated journey through West African markets and Spanish galleries, brought to the heart of Lille.

From African spices to Latino fire and Spanish flair, every dish feels like a journey. The balance between heat and flavor is just perfect; not just spicy for the sake of it, but rich, deep, and unforgettable.

Highly recommend for anyone who loves food with character and a kick!
